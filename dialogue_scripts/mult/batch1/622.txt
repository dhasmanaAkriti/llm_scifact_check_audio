Dr. Ellis: That’s a valid concern, Ms. Carter. But preemptive action without robust evidence can sometimes do more harm than good. For instance, vitamin D is crucial for bone health and the immune system.

Ms. Carter: I see. So, the disagreement here is really about how much weight we should give to this study in our personal health decisions?

Dr. Ellis: Exactly. It’s about balancing new findings with existing knowledge. We need to define this disagreement clearly to navigate our choices effectively.
