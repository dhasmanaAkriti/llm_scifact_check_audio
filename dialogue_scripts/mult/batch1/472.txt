Speaker 2: So, you're saying it's a starting point, not an endpoint?

Speaker 1: Exactly. It empowers researchers to explore new angles in aging and metabolic research. The implications extend beyond yeast; they could eventually influence how we understand aging in higher organisms.

Speaker 2: I see. While I'm skeptical of immediate applications, I appreciate the potential. It's crucial for the scientific community to remain curious yet cautious.
