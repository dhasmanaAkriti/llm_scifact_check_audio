Interpreter: And if we don't find consistent results?

Originator: Then we reassess. But the potential benefits far outweigh the risks. Imagine the advancements in treatments if lamins play a role in mitochondrial dynamics.

Interpreter: Agreed. So, the decision here is to allocate part of our resources to further investigate this claim. We should approach it cautiously but with an open mind.

Originator: Precisely. Let's draft a proposal for funding and see which collaborators we can bring on board.
