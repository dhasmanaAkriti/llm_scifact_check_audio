Dr. Patel: But isn't that an oversimplification? Homocysteine levels can be influenced by factors beyond B12, like folate and B6 levels, even genetics.

Dr. Patel: That’s a valid point, but if we're not careful, we risk oversimplifying the message. Patients might think B12 is a cure-all, which it isn't.

Dr. Lewis: Agreed, the message must be balanced. However, defining this disagreement helps us refine our public health communications. We need to highlight the role of B12 without downplaying other factors.

Dr. Patel: So, a nuanced message, then? We acknowledge B12's role but ensure people understand the multifactorial nature of homocysteine regulation.

Dr. Lewis: Exactly. We owe it to our patients to provide clarity without causing unnecessary alarm.
