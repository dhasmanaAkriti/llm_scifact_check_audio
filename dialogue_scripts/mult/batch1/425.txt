Dr. Ellis: Exactly. With enhanced monitoring, we could potentially mitigate future complications.

Dr. Kim: But wouldn't this require additional resources and training for our staff?

Dr. Ellis: It would, but the long-term benefits could outweigh the initial costs. We must decide if we can afford not to act.

Dr. Kim: True. The data is compelling. Perhaps a pilot program could help us assess the feasibility and impact.

Dr. Ellis: That’s an excellent idea. We could start with a smaller cohort and evaluate the outcomes.

Dr. Kim: Let's draft a proposal for the board. If we act now, we might give these children a better start.
