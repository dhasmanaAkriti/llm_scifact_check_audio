Speaker 3: I'm not dismissing it, Dr. Ellis. I'm advocating for rigorous validation. Before we accept JMJD6's role as definitive, we must ensure the findings are universally applicable.

Speaker 1: It seems we have a classic scientific debate here. Dr. Ellis and Dr. Patel, thank you for mapping out the points of conflict. It's clear that while JMJD6's role is promising, further research is essential to bridge the gap between hypothesis and established fact.
