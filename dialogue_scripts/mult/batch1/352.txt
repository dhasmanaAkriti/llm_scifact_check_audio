Skeptic: That sounds like a paradigm shift. But before we start rewriting textbooks, shouldn't we consider potential implications for ongoing research? How does this affect our current projects?

Advocate: That's why we're here. We need to decide whether to pivot some of our focus to explore these variations more deeply. It could open new avenues for understanding evolutionary mechanisms.

Skeptic: I see the potential, but is it worth redirecting our resources? We need a solid plan to ensure this isn't just a wild goose chase.

Advocate: Agreed. I propose we start with a pilot study to verify these findings further. If the results are consistent, we can expand our research scope.
